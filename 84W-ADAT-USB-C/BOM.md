# Bill of Materials - ADAT to USB-C Interface Setup

## Required Components

### 1. USB-C Audio Interface with ADAT
Choose one of the following based on your budget and requirements:

| Interface | Price Range | ADAT I/O | Pros | Cons |
|-----------|------------|----------|------|------|
| **Focusrite Scarlett 18i20 (3rd Gen)** | ~$550 | 2x ADAT I/O | Excellent Mac support, reliable drivers | Larger desktop unit |
| **Audient iD44** | ~$700 | 2x ADAT I/O | Premium preamps, solid build | Higher price |
| **PreSonus Studio 1810c** | ~$450 | 1x ADAT I/O | Affordable, good features | Single ADAT pair only |
| **RME Babyface Pro FS** | ~$750 | 1x ADAT I/O | Best-in-class drivers, portable | Premium price |
| **MOTU M4** | ~$250 | Optical I/O | Very compact, budget-friendly | Requires adapter for ADAT |

*Prices approximate as of 2024*

### 2. ADAT Optical Cables

| Item | Quantity | Specs | Price Range |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| Toslink Optical Cable | 2 | 3-6 ft length | $10-25 each |

**Recommended brands:**
- Cable Matters Toslink Cable
- AmazonBasics Digital Optical Audio Cable
- Hosa ODL-312 (professional grade)

**Notes:**
- You need 2 cables (one for send, one for return)
- Avoid cables longer than 15 feet to prevent signal degradation
- Metal connectors are more durable than plastic

### 3. USB-C Cable

| Item | Quantity | Specs | Price Range |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| USB-C Cable | 1 | USB 2.0 or higher, 3-6 ft | $10-20 |

**Notes:**
- Most interfaces include a USB-C cable
- USB 2.0 bandwidth is sufficient for ADAT (8 channels)
- Quality matters - cheap cables can cause dropouts

### 4. Expert Sleepers ES-10 (Assumed Already Owned)

| Item | Quantity | Specs | Price Range |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| Expert Sleepers ES-10 | 1 | 8-channel ADAT DC-coupled I/O | ~$340 |

## Compatibility Notes

All listed interfaces are:
- ✓ macOS compatible (tested with macOS 10.14+)
- ✓ Class compliant or have Mac drivers
- ✓ Support 44.1/48 kHz ADAT
- ✓ USB-C connectivity
- ✓ Low-latency performance suitable for live use

## Not Included

This BOM does not include:
- Computer (Mac with USB-C port)
- DAW software (Ableton Live, Logic Pro, etc.)
- Eurorack case and power supply
- Eurorack modules beyond ES-10
- Studio monitors or headphones

## Maintenance

- Keep optical cable connectors clean and dust-free
- Store optical cables carefully to avoid bending
- Update interface firmware periodically
- Clean USB connectors with compressed air
